Privacy Policy for FullDownloader

Last updated: February 02, 2026

Developer: CodePump

This Privacy Policy explains how FullDownloader ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Third-Party Login and Cookie Usage (X / Pixiv)

Important: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your login credentials (usernames or passwords) to our servers.

The App provides a built-in browser (WebView) feature that allows you to log in to third-party services, specifically X (formerly Twitter) and Pixiv. This feature is optional and is designed solely to help you access and download media from your personal lists (e.g., "Likes" or "Bookmarks").

When you log in via the WebView:

  • Direct Connection: You are logging in directly to the official websites of X or Pixiv. The App acts as a browser.
  • Cookies: The App accesses the session Cookies generated by your login locally on your device.
  • Purpose: These Cookies are used strictly to authenticate your requests to fetch your bookmarked/liked media lists for downloading purposes.
  • No External Storage: Your login credentials and authentication Cookies are NOT uploaded to CodePump's servers or shared with any other third parties. They remain on your device.

2. Data Collection via Google AdMob

This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google Inc.

Google AdMob may collect and use the following data to provide personalized ads and analyze traffic:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and other technical identifiers.
  • Advertising ID: A unique ID for advertising (Google Advertising ID) used to serve relevant ads.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with the ads (e.g., impressions, clicks).

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting the settings on your Android device (Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID).

3. Storage Permissions

The App requires access to your device's storage (Read/Write permissions) to save the downloaded media files to your device. We do not access other personal files on your device unrelated to the App's functionality.

4. Links to Third-Party Sites & Content Restrictions

The App allows you to download content from specific third-party websites (e.g., X and Pixiv).

Important Disclaimer: In compliance with Google Play Developer Policies and copyright regulations, this App does NOT support downloading videos from YouTube or any copyrighted music files.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review the privacy policies of X (Twitter) and Pixiv directly.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our records.
